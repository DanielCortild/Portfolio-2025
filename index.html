Daniel Cortild

Hi, I’m Daniel!

I am currently a MSc student in Applied Mathematics at the University of Groningen, member of the Systems, Control and Optimization group under the supervision of Juan Peypouquet, graduating in June 2025. I currently focus on stochastic gradient methods and their convergence properties. Previously, I obtained a BSc in Mathematics from the University of Groningen, where I specialised in Numerical Analysis.

Research Interests: Convex Optimization, Global Optimization, Fixed-Point Iterations, Computer-Aided Proofs
